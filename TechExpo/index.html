About the Technology

What is Thermoelectric Nano Paint?

This special paint uses tiny materials called thermoelectric nanomaterials that can turn heat from the sun or air into electricity. It works by using temperature differences between the inside and outside of walls to generate power, just by being painted onto buildings.

Key Features

Passive Energy Generation

Generates electricity quietly without any moving parts, simply from heat differences between indoors and outdoors.

Invisible & Aesthetic Friendly

Looks just like regular paint and can be applied in many colors without affecting building appearance.

Low Maintenance

Designed to last for many years with minimal upkeep, reducing the need for frequent repainting.
